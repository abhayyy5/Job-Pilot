What is Job Pilot?

Job Pilot is a modern job portal web application built to connect job seekers with employers efficiently. Explore full-time, part-time, or remote opportunities through an intuitive, user-friendly platform. Open-source contributions in GirlScript Summer of Code (GSSoC) focus on performance, clean design, and inclusivity.

Tech Stack

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Firebase, Font Awesome, Google Fonts (Poppins)

Key Features

Responsive UI, Navigation, Firebase Authentication (In Progress), Search & Filter Options

Goal

Make job hunting simple, transparent, and accessible while connecting talent with opportunity.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be the most trusted job platform globally, empowering talent to find meaningful opportunities easily.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between job seekers and employers by providing an intuitive, transparent, and inclusive platform.
